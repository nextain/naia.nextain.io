## Service Scope

naia.nextain.io is a service portal for Naia OS account authentication, billing, credit, and usage management.

## Account and Billing

Paid plans are processed via LemonSqueezy, and subscription status is synchronized with the portal. Payment methods and invoicing follow LemonSqueezy policies.

## Usage Restrictions

Account or API access may be restricted if service abuse, security risks, or policy violations are identified.
