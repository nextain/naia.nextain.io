Naia can be installed in two ways. Each is a completely independent method — choose the one that fits your situation.

> Want to try before installing? **[2. Naia OS Live USB](/en/manual/live-usb)** lets you experience Naia from just a USB drive.

## Naia OS — Full OS Installation (Recommended)

Boot from a USB drive and install directly to your hard drive. Naia is deeply integrated into the OS and launches automatically on every boot.

- **What you need**: A USB drive (8 GB+), a PC, and 64 GB+ of disk space.

**[View the Naia OS Installation Guide →](/en/manual/install-iso)**

## Linux App Installation

Install Naia as a standalone app on your existing Linux system. No need to reinstall your OS.

- Supports Flatpak, AppImage, DEB, and RPM.

**[View the App Installation Guide →](/en/manual/install-app)**
